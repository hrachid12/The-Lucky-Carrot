About Lucky

Welcome! My name is Lucky! I am a three year old pomeranian from New Jersey. I live with the most amaaaaazing family ever. I have three brothers and two sisters (all humans). I have many favorite activities. I love going out on drives and looking out the window. I love going out for walks (not even the cold can stop me!1) I just put on my hat and scarf and I'm ready to go! My most favorite is a lazy day where I just lay back and get scratches from everyone in my family. I also love playing with all of my toys. My favorite toys are tennis balls, squeaky toys, tennis balls, crinkle toys, oh and did I say tennis balls!!!

I'm also really good at keeping dangerous things away from our home. I'll sometimes watch outside a window and wait until I see something suspicious and I scare them away with my ferocious roars! I make sure I'm loud enough so everyone can hear me!!! I guess you're wondering why my website is called 'The Lucky Carrot'. Well, I REALLY love carrots. I love carrots. They're my favorite snack, my favorite toys are carrot shapes, and my favorite foods have carrots in them. I loveeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeee carrots!! I can't get enough of them! Anyway, I hope you love my blog as much as I love blogging. And all of my pictures are available for you to download. Just click the tiny picture of me!
